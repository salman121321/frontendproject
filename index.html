Tokenomics

Crypto Asset Partnership CREAT'OR has meticulously structured the allocation of its 500,000,000 tokens to ensure the long-term sustainability and strategic growth of the platform. The tokens are divided into distinct segments, each serving a critical purpose within the CREAT’OR ecosyste

Pre-Sale (25%):

100,000,000 tokens have been allocated for the Pre-Sale phase, offering early adopters and supporters a chance to participate in CREAT’OR's vision from the ground up.

Listing & Staking (21%):

Another 100,000,000 tokens are earmarked for Listing & Staking, ensuring ample liquidity on exchanges, and rewarding users who stake their tokens.

Team (20%):

This portion is set aside for the dedicated team behind CREAT’OR, amounting to 100,000,000 tokens that represent their commitment and ongoing development efforts.

Marketing (5%):

50,000,000 tokens are allocated for marketing initiatives, playing a crucial role in CREAT’OR's outreach and user acquisition strategies.

Reserve & Treasury (17%):

90,000,000 tokens form the Reserve & Treasury segment, a strategic reserve to support future development and unforeseen expenses.

Burn Event (5%):

To enhance the token's value and scarcity, 25,000,000 tokens are reserved to be burned, particularly the unsold tokens after the Pre-Sale.

Legal & Advisory (4%):

20,000,000 tokens are allocated for legal and advisory purposes, ensuring CREAT’OR's operations remain compliant and strategically sound.

Partnerships (3%):

Finally, 15,000,000 tokens are allocated to foster partnerships that will augment CREAT’OR's ecosystem and user experience.

Burn Policy:

In line with our commitment to a robust economic model, 5% of unsold tokens will be burned upon the conclusion of the Pre-Sale, reducing the total supply, and potentially increasing the value for existing token holders.

CREAT’OR's Tokenomics are designed to balance immediate operational needs with long-term strategic growth. By aligning token distribution with our goals, we ensure that each stakeholder group is appropriately incentivized and that the CREAT’OR ecosystem is positioned for success.
